Our Philosophy

At Nawab Fragrances, we blend tradition with innovation. Each fragrance is handcrafted using premium essential oils and aged to perfection. Explore our signature collections and find your signature scent.

We are dedicated to crafting luxury fragrances that tell a story, evoke emotions, and leave a lasting impression. Experience the art of perfumery with us, where every bottle holds a piece of our passion and expertise.

Our Journey Through Scent

2021: Brand Founded

Nawab Fragrances was born from a passion for exquisite aromas and a vision to blend traditional craftsmanship with modern elegance.

2022: First Best-Seller Launched

"Rose Vintage" quickly became a customer favorite, establishing our reputation for quality and sophistication across the region.

2023: Expanded to International Markets

Our unique fragrances began reaching connoisseurs globally, spreading the Nawab Fragrances experience far and wide, affirming our international appeal.

2024: Awarded "Best Niche Perfume"

Recognized by industry experts for our innovative blends and unwavering commitment to exceptional quality, a true testament to our dedication.

2025: Expanded Internationally

Entered new international markets and opened our first flagship boutique, strengthening our global presence while continuing to innovate with exclusive, handcrafted fragrances.

Founded by master perfumer Fahad Khan Sherwani, Nawab Fragrances embarked on a mission to redefine luxury fragrances. Fahad's deep understanding of raw materials and his artistic flair led to the creation of scents that are not just perfumes, but experiences. From the carefully sourced rare oud to the delicate extraction of floral essences, every step in our process is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality and artistry. We believe that a fragrance should be an extension of one's personality, a silent statement of elegance and individuality.

Our dedication extends beyond the bottle. We are committed to ethical sourcing, sustainable practices, and creating products that are both kind to the planet and luxurious for our customers. Join us on this aromatic journey and discover the essence of timeless beauty, handcrafted with passion.

Fahad Khan Sherwani

Founder & Master Perfumer

"Fragrance is the art of memories, bottled."

Fahad, with his profound expertise in olfactory arts, established Nawab Fragrances with the vision of creating scents that resonate deeply. His passion for blending rare notes and creating unique compositions is at the heart of every Nawab Fragrances creation, ensuring each bottle is a masterpiece.
